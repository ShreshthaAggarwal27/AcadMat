FAQs

> Can I donate books to the website?
Absolutely! We encourage book donations to support our community. You can donate books by going to the "Donate" section and following the instructions to submit your donation details.
> How do I contact the seller?
You can contact the seller by clicking on their profile and using the "Contact Seller" option. You can discuss item details,payment, or shipping arrangements through this platform.
> Do you offer international shipping?
The availability of international shipping depends on the seller's preferences. Some sellers may offer international shipping options, while others may only ship domestically.
> What happens if I receive a book in a condition different from the description?
If you receive an item that doesn't match seller's description, please contact our customer support within 2-3 days of receiving the item, and we will assist you in resolving the issue.
> What if I have more questions?
Then we're standing by to help. Contact us, and we'll do our best to help.
